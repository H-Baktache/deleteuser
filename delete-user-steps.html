Account Deletion Guide

BIBA - Complete Step-by-Step Account Deletion Process

Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. This action will permanently remove your account and all associated data from our system, including your profile, applications, CV files, and all other user data. Please ensure you have backed up any important information before proceeding.

Step-by-Step Account Deletion Process

1

Navigate to Profile Section

Open the BIBA app and navigate to your profile section. You can find this by tapping on the profile icon or navigating to the "Profile" tab in the bottom navigation menu.

Figure 1: Navigate to the Profile section
2

Locate Delete Account Option

Scroll down to the bottom of your profile page. You will find a "Delete Account" button, typically colored red to indicate it's a destructive action. This button is usually located at the bottom of the profile settings.

Figure 2: Locate the Delete Account button
3

Confirmation Modal Appears

Tap the "Delete Account" button. A confirmation modal will appear asking you to confirm your decision. This modal serves as your final chance to cancel the deletion process before proceeding.

Figure 3: Confirmation modal with text input field
4

Type Confirmation Text

In the confirmation modal, you will see a text input field. Type "DELETE" (in all capital letters) to confirm that you understand this action is permanent and cannot be undone. This additional confirmation step ensures you are fully aware of the consequences.

Figure 4: Type "DELETE" in the confirmation field
5

Account Successfully Deleted

Once you type "DELETE" and tap the confirm button, the system will process your account deletion. You will see a success message confirming that your account has been permanently deleted from our system.

Figure 5: Account deletion success confirmation

What Happens During Account Deletion

  • Profile Removal: Your complete profile and personal information are permanently deleted
  • Application Data: All your job applications and associated data are removed
  • Document Cleanup: Your CV files and uploaded documents are permanently deleted
  • Activity Clearance: All notifications, activity history, and user data are cleared
  • Authentication Removal: Your account is permanently removed from Supabase Auth system
  • Automatic Logout: You will be logged out and redirected to the login screen
